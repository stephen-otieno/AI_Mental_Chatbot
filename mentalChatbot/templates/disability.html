Disability and Stigma

Disability is a natural part of human diversity, yet people with disabilities often face societal barriers and misconceptions that lead to exclusion and discrimination. Stigma remains a significant challenge, affecting their ability to participate fully in social, economic, and cultural life. Many individuals with disabilities encounter prejudice, lack of accessibility, and institutional challenges that hinder their progress. Addressing these issues requires awareness, advocacy, and inclusive policies to ensure equal opportunities for all.
